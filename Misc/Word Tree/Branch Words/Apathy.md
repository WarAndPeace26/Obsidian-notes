# Apathy
1. Lack of interest
	- *He soon fell into a kind of apathy.*

**ORIGIN:**
> early 17th century: from French _apathie_, via Latin from Greek _apatheia_, from **apathēs|| ‘without feeling’**, from **_a-_ ‘without’ + pathos ||‘suffering’**.
